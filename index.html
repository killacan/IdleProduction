Hello and welcome to the Game! This is an Idle factory game. The Gameplay can seem pretty complicated so I will try to beak it down simply.

  • First your factory is going to need power, so build a windmill to get things going. This will also give you a little extra money per 100 extra power you have.
  • Next you are going to want to build a Iron Mine. Just click on the Iron Mine building in the build menu on the left side of the screen.
  • After you click on it, the information shows up in the selected buildings window on the right.
  • Then just click anywhere on the grid to build the building. If you have extra power it will automatically make Resources for you.
  • Lastly just click the sell goods button to make some money.This money you can spend on more buildings.
  • Early production chain is going to look like this, Iron Mine -> Iron Smelter -> Steel Mill.
  • The rest of the game is for you to discover. Have Fun!
