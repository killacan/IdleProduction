Hello and welcome to the game! This is an idle game where to goal is to build buildings. Those buildings collect resources which you can sell. Later buildings will consume resources created by early buildings. Click on the building on the left and that will "select" the building for you. Then click on the grid to build the building. To get started it might be a good idea to build a few Iron Mines. The market is a special building that will increase the value of goods sold. That's it! have fun.
